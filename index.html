Questions

Real questions.
Straight
answers.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

Contact Us
What ages is camp for?+
Camp welcomes kids ages 3–12. Activities and cooking stations are adapted by age so every camper can fully participate and succeed.
What are the camp hours?+
Camp runs 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM daily. Optional Before & After Care extends the day from 8:30 AM until 3:30 PM.
Why only one week?+
One week is exactly enough to make it magical. We pack everything — cooking, trips, Jewish pride, friendships — into five unforgettable days. Exclusive by design.
How does drop-off and pickup work?+
When you arrive, a staff member will greet your child. At pickup, only individuals listed on your registration form are authorized to collect your camper — please have ID ready.
Will there be security on site?+
Yes. Camp will have security presence during camp hours, along with staff-supervised drop-off, pickup, and all activities.
What should my child bring?+
Comfortable clothes they don't mind getting messy (aprons are provided), a water bottle, sunscreen, any allergy medications, and a great appetite. We take care of everything else.
My child has food allergies — is camp safe?+
We take allergies seriously. Note all allergies during registration. Our staff reviews every camper's needs. For severe allergies, we'll speak with you directly before camp begins.
Are scholarships available?+
We believe every Jewish child deserves this experience. Limited scholarship assistance is available on a first-come, first-served basis. Contact rabbi@jewishpedro.org before registering to apply.
🔒 Camper Safety: Only individuals listed on your registration form are authorized for pickup.
